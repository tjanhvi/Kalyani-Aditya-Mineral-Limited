Welcome to Kalyani Aditya Mineral Limited.

We are a leading coal trading firm that has been serving clients across the country. Over the years, we have built a reputation for providing high-quality coal at competitive prices, and our commitment to customer satisfaction has helped us to become a trusted name in the industry.

Our company was founded with the aim of providing reliable and efficient coal trading services to industries, power plants, and businesses across India. We started small, but with the dedication of our team, we have grown to become one of the most respected names in the business.
