About Us

Welcome to Kalyani Aditya Mineral Limited, a leading coal trading company dedicated to providing high-quality coal products and exceptional services to meet the energy needs of our clients. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for coal procurement and distribution. We understand the vital role that coal plays in powering industries and driving economic growth. As a result, we are committed to sourcing and delivering the finest-grade coal products to our customers, ensuring reliability, efficiency, and sustainability in every transaction.

Our team comprises industry experts who possess extensive knowledge and expertise in coal trading. We meticulously assess the quality and specifications of the coal we trade to ensure it meets the stringent standards and requirements of our clients. Whether you are in need of thermal coal, metallurgical coal, or any other specific coal type, we have the resources and capabilities to fulfill your demands.
We take pride in our strong network of suppliers and partners, which enables us to source coal from reputable mines and producers across the globe. This strategic collaboration allows us to offer a diverse range of coal options tailored to your specific requirements. Our procurement process emphasizes transparency, fair trade practices, and ethical sourcing to maintain the highest levels of integrity in our operations.

At Kalyani Aditya Mineral Limited, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to build long-term relationships with our clients by providing personalized solutions, competitive pricing, and timely delivery. Our dedicated logistics team ensures smooth and efficient transportation of coal, optimizing supply chain management to meet your deadlines and expectations. Whether you are a power generation company, a steel manufacturer, or an industrial player seeking a reliable coal supply, Kalyani Aditya Mineral Ltd. is your trusted partner. We invite you to explore our comprehensive range of coal products and experience our unmatched commitment to quality, reliability, and customer service.

MISSION & VISION

We are trusted as a highly reliable source for uninterrupted supply of quality, cheap product which is critical to the client's entire operations. Our predefined processes, policies and dispute resolution mechanisms, make us unbiased in our approach to any situation Our achievements so far have been underpinned by a great team of enthusiastic experts whose deep knowledge of the international coal market is a fantastic asset.

Kalyani Aditya Minerals Private Limited is a nationally recognised coal trading and coal exchange company with a diversified distribution network across the globe. We specialise in providing premium quality products and services to our clients.

We are building ourselves to be one of the most trusted coal traders across India. The company is engaged in bulk trading primarily with USA Steam Coal, Russian Coal, Indonesian Coal, Australian Coal, etc, directly with reputed coal miners only.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission Is To Create And Grow Long-Term Value For Our Stakeholders By Implementing Best Practices In Coal Trading And Increasing Customer Satisfaction.

This improves our value proposition to our customer base. The company as a team is committed to developing a culture of sustainable business practices, equality, and multiple opportunities for everyone around us. We are on the mission of providing best coal at competitive price to our traders and customers, and also provide good quality to our customers.

OUR VISION

At KAML, we are committed to being an entrepreneurial professional organisation that adapts quickly to the changing world. We are dedicated to becoming the global leader in the coal industry.
